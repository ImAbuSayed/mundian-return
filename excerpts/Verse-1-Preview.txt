Kaali raat ch aundi teri khushbu, sweet midnight scent,
Tere piche guzare pal saare, time beautifully spent.
Teri akhaan da nasha bura ae, dangerous addiction,
Dil mera karda fariyaad sachi, pure conviction.
Kalla main turda haan raahan te, neon lights blur,
Tere bina sab kuch lage sunna, a quiet whisper.
Hath mera palka ch phad lai kude, pull me so close,
Tera nakhra mainu roz maarda, my lovely rose.
