Dhuaan udhda mere thande saahan cho, breathing cold air,
Tere bina eh zindagi lagdi ae, totally unfair.
Lokaan diyaan nazran ton bacha ke tenu, keep you safe,
Mainu apna hamesha bana lai tu, end this chafe.
Meri khamoshi ander kardi shor, a silent scream,
Teri photo main roz takda haan, a fading dream.
Kise hor da tenu hon ni denda, marking my claim,
Tere ishq ch main rokh jalda haan, wild open flame.
