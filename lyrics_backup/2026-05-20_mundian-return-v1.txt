[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Nain tere kaale jadoo karde, slow my heartbeat,
Saanh mera achanak ruk janda, baby when we meet.
Raatan di nind tu lai gayi saari, leaving no trace,
Husan tera seene te karda vaar, with a dark grace.

[Hook 2]
Tere piche pagal main firda haan, every single night,
Hathan vich hath tu pa lai mera, holding me tight.
Akhiyan ch nasha tera bolda ae, shining so bright,
Aaja mere kol ni sohniye, make it all right.

[Verse 1]
Kaali raat ch aundi teri khushbu, sweet midnight scent,
Tere piche guzare pal saare, time beautifully spent.
Teri akhaan da nasha bura ae, dangerous addiction,
Dil mera karda fariyaad sachi, pure conviction.
Kalla main turda haan raahan te, neon lights blur,
Tere bina sab kuch lage sunna, a quiet whisper.
Hath mera palka ch phad lai kude, pull me so close,
Tera nakhra mainu roz maarda, my lovely rose.

[Pre-Chorus]
Teri doori mainu bada tadpave, making me weak,
Dil di gall main sach kenda haan, hear me speak.
Eh fasle mita de saare ajj, bridge the space,
Mere seene aa ke lag ja, in this dark place.

[Chorus]
Teri yaad ch main karda haan roam, under the stars,
Saanu de de tu thodi jehi thaa, healing my scars.
Dil mera dhadakda kalle hi, skipping a beat,
Teri ik takni hi kardi ae, turn up the heat.
Aaja tu hun meri baahan ch, end this cold war,
Tere bina main kakh rull janda, down to the core.
Tu meri ban ja ik raniye, my only queen,
Tere nakhre da jadoo chalda, like a movie scene.

[Verse 2]
Tere naina di goli sidhi vajji, sharp as a blade,
Duniya di parwah main chhad ditti, in the dark shade.
Kaale jacket de vich sada dhadke, wild heartbeat,
Teri bewafai ne ditti mainu, bitter defeat.
Zid te adeyan main kalla khada haan, holding my ground,
Teri awaz dhunda main har passe, a missing sound.
Wafa di kasma khaa ke ainvaye, breaking the rules,
Tere piche main kamla lagg gaya, acting like a fool.

[Verse 3]
Dhuaan udhda mere thande saahan cho, breathing cold air,
Tere bina eh zindagi lagdi ae, totally unfair.
Lokaan diyaan nazran ton bacha ke tenu, keep you safe,
Mainu apna hamesha bana lai tu, end this chafe.
Meri khamoshi ander kardi shor, a silent scream,
Teri photo main roz takda haan, a fading dream.
Kise hor da tenu hon ni denda, marking my claim,
Tere ishq ch main rokh jalda haan, wild open flame.

[Bridge]
Saeed karda fariyad bas teri, in the still night,
Akhiyan cho athru mere dulde, losing my sight.
Dil di aawaz meri tu sun lai, feel my pure pain,
Tere bina main sachi muk janda, fading in the rain.

[Final Chorus]
Teri yaad ch main karda haan roam, under the stars,
Saanu de de tu thodi jehi thaa, healing my scars.
Dil mera dhadakda kalle hi, skipping a beat,
Teri ik takni hi kardi ae, turn up the heat.
Aaja tu hun meri baahan ch, end this cold war,
Tere bina main kakh rull janda, down to the core.
Akhiyan de hanju mere dasde ne, true lonely signs,
Tere bina meri adhuri kismet, empty blank lines.
