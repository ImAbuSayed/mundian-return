Tere naina di goli sidhi vajji, sharp as a blade,
Duniya di parwah main chhad ditti, in the dark shade.
Kaale jacket de vich sada dhadke, wild heartbeat,
Teri bewafai ne ditti mainu, bitter defeat.
Zid te adeyan main kalla khada haan, holding my ground,
Teri awaz dhunda main har passe, a missing sound.
Wafa di kasma khaa ke ainvaye, breaking the rules,
Tere piche main kamla lagg gaya, acting like a fool.
