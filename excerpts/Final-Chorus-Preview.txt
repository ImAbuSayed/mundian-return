Teri yaad ch main karda haan roam, under the stars,
Saanu de de tu thodi jehi thaa, healing my scars.
Dil mera dhadakda kalle hi, skipping a beat,
Teri ik takni hi kardi ae, turn up the heat.
Aaja tu hun meri baahan ch, end this cold war,
Tere bina main kakh rull janda, down to the core.
Akhiyan de hanju mere dasde ne, true lonely signs,
Tere bina meri adhuri kismet, empty blank lines.
